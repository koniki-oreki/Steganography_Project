Steganography Tool

DESCRIPTION:

**
Steganography is like sending a hidden message in a way that only the intended receiver knows it's there.
**
There are several types of steganography, such as text, image, audio and video steganography.
**
Each type utilizes different mediums to conceal information, making it a versatile and covert method of communication.
**
The tool can hide a text message inside any type which would be imperceptible to the naked eye but detectable by someone who knows how to look for it.
**
The goal is to keep the existence of the hidden information unknown to anyone who isn't aware it's there.
**
This makes steganography a powerful tool for secure and private communication.
